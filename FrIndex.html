BIENVENUE

At Faraja, we strongly believe that harnessing the power of emerging technologies in teaching, learning and training is the keystone to the success of the innovation effort required in order to adequately educate and train next generations of professionals who are capable to live up to challenges resulting from the ongoing digital revolution. We are situated at the confluence of education, journalism, media arts instructional design and web technologies.


Jean-Marie Muhirwa, the founder of Faraja has explored all these channels throughout his academic and professional journey, over the last three decades. He has used his new html5, CSS and JavaScript skills to design and hand-code this website. It is destined to learners, educators, training professionals, policy makers and the wider public.

We strongly believe this is the way to go in order to harness the huge potential of new technologies and trigger the e-learning revolution. While the digital revolution has positively impacted many fields of activity over the last few decades, it has, by the same token steered modern economies and societies in new unchartered territories. We need to better harness and leverage its power in order to educate and train the future generations and equip them with the right skills that will allow them to live up to the emerging challenges.

We seek to build on our good theoretical and empirical understanding of technology-based learning in order to contribute to the emmergence of innovative ideas and best practices of design and development of learning materials informed by pedagogy and delivered by orchestrating media supports that are appropriate to target audiences and their learning environments.
